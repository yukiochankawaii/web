the importance of frends

In the COVID-19 pandemic

In 2021, my life dramatically changes. In February, I took an exam to enter the university. I graduated the high school and only I and my friends went to Osaka for the first time and started a part-time job in March. Then, I entered the Showa women's university and choose studying French as a second language in April. I made a lot of new friends in this university. However, no matter how I make many friends, I feel loneliness because I could not spend enough time to get along with my new friends and I avoided to hung out with my old friends as much as possible due to corona pandemic.

Then, I realize how my friends give me power. Just I cannot see my friends, that makes me so pessimistic. When I want to talk about my university life and listen to my friends' stories, I cannot see my friends. So, I was really depressed.

Thanks to this situation, I can find that talking with my friends face to face makes me happy. In 2022, I want to go many places with my friends.
